About Me

I am Nahid Hasan, an educator and researcher in Computer Science with a strong interest in Artificial Intelligence, particularly in Machine Learning and its applications in Computer Vision. My goal is to advance research in intelligent systems that can learn from data and interpret the world visually, while also inspiring and guiding students in the field of computing. I am passionate about exploring new ideas, contributing to impactful research, and growing within the global academic and research community.
